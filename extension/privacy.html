⬡ ContextLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What data we collect

ContextLens captures AI conversation turns (your messages and AI responses) from supported platforms including Claude.ai, ChatGPT, and Perplexity. This data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.

Where your data goes

By default, all data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server unless you explicitly configure a sync endpoint in the Settings tab. If you configure a sync server, your conversation turns are sent to that server for backup purposes.

What we do NOT do

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties. We do not require account creation. We do not track your usage for advertising purposes.

Optional server sync

If you use the optional Hetzner sync endpoint, conversation turns are stored in a PostgreSQL database on your configured server. You control this server and its data. The default sync endpoint (contextlens.65.108.217.183.nip.io) is provided as a convenience and stores minimal metadata.

Permissions used

storage — to save conversation memory locally. tabs — to open conversations when you click a memory turn. identity — reserved for future Drive sync feature. activeTab — to detect which AI platform you are using.
